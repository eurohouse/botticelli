# Eurohouse Botticelli

Get an aristocratic vibe with our digital art masterpiece collection.

"Elegance is the only beauty that never fades." - Audrey Hepburn

## Background

This music collection generally consists of classical, Old Hollywood and instrumental music from various artists which were discovered circa 2019.

## Composition

We ship within this package the following types of music:

* Old Hollywood motion pictures music (Audrey Hepburn, Marilyn Monroe)
* 20th classic and jazz songs (Frank Sinatra, Fred Astaire)
* Motion pictures themes (James Bond, Leaving Neverland)
* Motion pictures and video games soundtracks (Bart Delissen, James Horner)
* Video games themes (Civilization VI, Mafia Definitive Edition)
* Classical and baroque music (Johann Strauss, Tchaikovsky)
* Karaoke songs and audio samples (Karaoke, 7 Days with a Russian Beauty)
* 70's, 80's and 90's pop music (Eiffel 65, Michael Jackson)
* Modern pop music artists (Ariana Grande, T-PAIN)
* Chill out, lo-fi and lounge music (LAKEY INSPIRED, Nathan Allen)
* Celebrity impersonators (Michael Trapson/Michael Jackson)
